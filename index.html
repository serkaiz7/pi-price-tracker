About Pi Network

Pi Network, pioneered in 2019 by a team of Stanford graduates, is an innovative cryptocurrency initiative aimed at making digital currency accessible to everyone. Boasting a community of over 40 million engaged users as of March 2025, Pi leverages a unique, energy-efficient mining process via mobile devices, utilizing the Stellar Consensus Protocol (SCP) to minimize environmental impact.

Currently in its enclosed mainnet phase, Pi Network is gearing up for a highly anticipated open mainnet launch in mid-2025, pending the completion of KYC verification and Pi coin migration for its user base. This platform is designed to empower a decentralized economy, offering tools for peer-to-peer transactions, a growing marketplace, and robust support for blockchain developers.
