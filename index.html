About Pi Network

Pi Network, launched in 2019 by Stanford PhDs, is a transformative cryptocurrency platform designed to democratize digital currency access. With over 35 million users globally, it enables mobile-based mining through an eco-friendly consensus mechanism based on the Stellar Consensus Protocol (SCP), eliminating the need for energy-intensive hardware.

Currently in its enclosed mainnet phase (as of March 2025), Pi Network is preparing for an open mainnet launch, requiring users to complete KYC and migrate their mined Pi. The platform aims to foster a decentralized ecosystem with real-world applications, including peer-to-peer transactions, marketplace integrations, and developer tools.
