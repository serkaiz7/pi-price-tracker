About Pi Network

Pi Network is a pioneering cryptocurrency and developer platform launched in 2019 by a team of Stanford PhDs. Designed to democratize digital currency, it enables users to mine Pi coins via a mobile app, eliminating the need for energy-intensive hardware. This eco-friendly approach has attracted over 35 million users worldwide, known as Pioneers.

The platform leverages the Stellar Consensus Protocol (SCP) to create a decentralized ecosystem for transactions, app development, and a global digital economy. Currently in its enclosed mainnet phase (as of early 2025), Pi Network is preparing for an open mainnet launch, requiring users to complete KYC and migrate their mined Pi.

Pi Network’s vision includes real-world utility through peer-to-peer transactions, marketplace applications, and developer tools, positioning it as a leader in accessible blockchain technology. Track its price trends above and support the ecosystem below.
